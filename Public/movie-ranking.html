The Best Movies According to Anil Kambar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kantara

Kantara (Mystical Forest) is a 2022 Indian Kannada-language action thriller film, written and directed by Rishab Shetty.

K.G.F Chapter 1 & 2

KGF is an Indian Kannada language period action film series, directed by Prashanth Neel

Babruvahana

Babruvahana is a 1977 Indian Kannada-language Hindu mythological film directed by Hunsur Krishnamurthy.
